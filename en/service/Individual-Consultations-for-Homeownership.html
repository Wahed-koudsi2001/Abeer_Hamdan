What is a Personal Real Estate Ownership Consultation?

A personal real estate ownership consultation provides guidance and support to individuals regarding their properties, including buying, selling, managing, or improving them. These consultations involve assessing the property’s potential, advising on its value, and managing sales or leasing transactions. This type of consultation can also address complex issues such as dividing inherited properties or resolving disputes related to property ownership arrangements.
